How To Play?

wordle is a simple word game
all you have to do is put a random word and the game will check it
and depend on the color of the box of the letter you can know if :

if the box color change to gray the letter is not in the word

if the box color change to yellow the letter is in the word but not in the right place

and if the box color change to green the letter is in the word and in the right place
and down on the keyboard you can see the letters you have used
